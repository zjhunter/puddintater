Archives: Lockie Hunter
A long time resident of the American South and of San Francisco, Lockie Hunter now lives in Boston where she is enrolled as an MFA student in the creative writing program at Emerson College. She is that girl on the beach with her nose stuck in a book and is currently working on a Southern novel in hopes of preserving the eccentricities of her family and the region. You can reach her at lockie_hunter@Emerson.edu.
