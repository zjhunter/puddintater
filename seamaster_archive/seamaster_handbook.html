General Information

Unit 111 SeaMaster
1310 North Waccamaw, Garden City, S.C. 29576.
(843) 651-8434

Emergencies: Dial 911
For a problem with the unit please call Mark Noll at (843) 457-6671.
Media Center:
The unit is equipped with a television, VCR and Stereo/CD/DVD player. To operate the VCR, please follow instructions on the dog. The CD player also plays DVDs. Simply place a DVD in the cd tray, turn the TV to channel 3 and push play on the remote. Volume is controlled on the stereo system.
Telephone:
The number here is (843) 651-8434. All incoming and local calls are free. Please use a personal calling card to make long distance telephone calls and please do not accept collect calls.
Cleaning:
Please leave the unit as you found it. There are cleaning supplies underneath the sinks in all the bathrooms and under the kitchen sink as well. The vacuum is in the living room closet. Please also dispose of any food in the fridge and freezer when you leave. If you prefer to have someone else do the work, cleaning services are listed on the fridge.
Reservations:
You can contact us via email at seamaster@puddintater.com. Check out the SeaMaster web page as well at www.puddintater.com. Click on the SeaMaster logo.
